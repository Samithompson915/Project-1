About Us

RCA is a church in Riverside working through God to build a better community. Everyone is welcome here to hear the word of God. The church was founded by Pastor Drew Brattrud and his wife, Pastor Jeannie, in 2005. The church is like a home to Pastor Drew, his family, and the church members. Go to the "Service Times" link to see the service times and join us! We look forward to meeting you!
